About Us

The Nur al-Yateem Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing care, support, and opportunities for orphans around the world. Founded on principles of compassion and solidarity, the foundation aims to address the unique needs and challenges faced by orphaned children, ensuring they receive love, protection, education, and access to essential resources. Through a range of programs and initiatives, including shelter provision, educational support, healthcare assistance, and vocational training, Nur al-Yateem Foundation strives to empower orphaned children to lead fulfilling lives and contribute positively to their communities. Our Mission

The mission of Nur al-Yateem Foundation is to provide love, protection, education, and essential resources to orphaned children, empowering them to lead fulfilling lives and contribute positively to society.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs including shelter provision, educational support, healthcare assistance, and vocational training, designed to meet the unique needs of orphaned children and facilitate their holistic development.

Get Involved
